### 3Ô∏è‚É£ Customer Revenue Concentration (Pareto Analysis)
**Business Question**  
> Is revenue concentrated among a small group of customers?

**Approach**
- Loaded customer-level sales data into Python
- Calculated cumulative revenue contribution
- Built a Pareto (80/20) analysis chart

**Key Insights**
- Revenue is not extremely concentrated among a very small group of customers.
- Approximately 80% of total revenue is generated by ~25‚Äì30% of customers, not the classic 20%.
- This indicates a moderately diversified revenue base, reducing dependency risk on a handful of top customers.
- While top customers remain important, broad customer retention and mid-tier customer growth are equally critical for sustaining revenue.
- The business is less exposed to single-customer churn risk, but optimization opportunities exist in uplifting mid-value customers.
